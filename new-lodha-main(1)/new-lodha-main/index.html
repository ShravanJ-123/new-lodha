Overview

Building Dreams, Creating Homes:

Welcome to the Lodha Wakad a New Launch by Lodha Group at Wakad, a premium residential development crafted for those who seek the finest in life. Perfectly positioned in one of Pune’s most vibrant and connected neighborhoods, this project offers a seamless blend of modernity and elegance. With its strategic location on Wakad-Bhosari BRTS Road, the development ensures effortless connectivity to Hinjewadi IT Hub, Baner, Balewadi, and major business corridors. Surrounded by top-tier schools, healthcare facilities, and buzzing retail destinations, it brings unmatched convenience to your doorstep. Thoughtfully designed for elite living, this project stands as a beacon of luxury, exclusivity, and sophistication in the heart of Wakad.
